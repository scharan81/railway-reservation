About


Now a days cancellation and rebooking of tickets may cost in many service charges which causes a lot of inconvenience (regarding money)
to the users .Not only this but also when there is an emergency issue occurs while travelling in the train (like criminal offences and
medical emergency),they cant reach the officials or get the required help immediately.
They can only rectify their problem only after reaching their destination/next station.
By this project we are trying to overcome the above mentioned issues.
---> To overcome the cancellation and rebooking issue we have added a new feature named "Shift_Tickets".
This feature helps us to shift our tickets to another required date of journey if tickets are available for
that day or else we can cancel the tickets.This helps the user by applying minimum charges.
---> To overcome the emergency issues we are providing a feature named "Complaint" which includes
both Reservation as well as Emergency sections.When there is an emergency , user/traveller can mention
their issues in the emergency section which will reach the officials in time and can send the required
support to the next possible station itself.This will help travellers for a safe and comfortable journey.
