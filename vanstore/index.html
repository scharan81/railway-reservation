ABOUT VAN STORE    

Van store aims to improve access to vegetables & groceries for people living in areas with limited access to shops or markets & also saves their time.It provides vegetables and groceries at a low cost by street trading.It offers oppurtunites for people as paid workers & creates employement.The vegetables and groceries in the van have been set up with short term funding from different sources. Every person can understand and access the vanstore .It stores the vegetables & groceries in the van according to the area that they sell for the day.It maintains the quality of vegetables & groceries to have the customer base.The van trades every day to different areas as scheduled.Supplying other venues helps in benefitting customers as there is less crowd and can peacefully select their cart!!
